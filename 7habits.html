The 7 Habits

Click any habit to explore what it means ↓

1
Be Proactive
🚀
▼

Take responsibility for your life. Your behaviour is a function of your decisions, not your conditions. Focus on what you can control — your Circle of Influence — rather than your Circle of Concern.

2
Begin with the End in Mind
🎯
▼

Start each day, task, or project with a clear vision of your direction and destination. Define your personal mission statement so every action is guided by a purpose bigger than the moment.

3
Put First Things First
📅
▼

Prioritise and execute your most important (not just urgent) tasks. Use the Time Management Matrix to spend more energy in Quadrant II — important, non-urgent activities that build your future.

4
Think Win-Win
🤝
▼

Seek mutual benefit in all interactions. Life is not a competition — there is plenty for everybody. Win-Win isn't just nice, it's the only sustainable approach to long-term relationships and agreements.

5
Seek First to Understand
👂
▼

Listen empathetically before seeking to be understood. Most people listen with the intent to reply, not to understand. Empathic listening builds trust and opens the door to genuine influence.

6
Synergise
✨
▼

The whole is greater than the sum of its parts. Valuing differences — in perspective, background, and ideas — creates collaborative solutions that no individual could achieve alone.

7
Sharpen the Saw
🌱
▼

Preserve and enhance your greatest asset — you. Regularly renew your physical, social, mental, and spiritual dimensions. Continuous self-improvement is the foundation that makes all other habits possible.
